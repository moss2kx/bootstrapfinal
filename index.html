About Us

At Focus + Frame Photography, we believe that photography is not just about taking pictures, but about capturing life's moments and preserving them for generations to come. We're committed to providing our clients with a personalized and enjoyable photography experience, and we look forward to working with you to capture your special moments.

Whether you're looking for family portraits, wedding photography, senior photos, or any other type of photography service, we're here to help. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision and preferences, and we use our technical skills and artistic eye to create images that exceed their expectations.

Weddings

Your wedding day is one of the most special days of your life, and at Focus & Frame Photography, we're committed to capturing every moment and detail of your big day. Our experienced and professional photographers have a keen eye for capturing the essence of your love story, and we use our technical skills and creativity to produce stunning images that you'll treasure for a lifetime.

We offer a range of wedding photography packages to suit your needs and preferences, from engagement sessions and bridal portraits to full-day coverage of your wedding ceremony and reception. Our packages also include digital copies of your photos, as well as options for prints, albums, and other custom products.
